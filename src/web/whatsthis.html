What is Drop.Info?

## Welcome to Drop.Info! We built this website so you can share anything that can fit into an index card with just a short link. Here's a video of how it works:

Now that that's out of the way, let us show what you can do with our app.
## Making a Card Everything you share on Drop.Info comes in the form of a **card**, which is like a post. To create a card, just go to our [home page](http://drop-info.live/). Every card needs a **title** and **contents**. You can put whatever you want in the contents of your card and it will be beautifully displayed in *Markdown*, a text format used in blogging. You can also upload an image and *constrain the text*, which means keeping it in the middle of the page.
## Markdown Tips To preview your post in markdown, click the **eye icon** above the contents textbox. Here's some Markdown basics: (**Tip**: Leave an empty line between every line of text you write!)

Header

` # Header ` ## Small Header ` ## Small Header ` ### Even Smaller Header ` ### Even Smaller Header `
*italicized text*: `*italicized text*` **bold text**: `**bold text**` [Link](http://drop-info.live): `[Link](http://drop-info.live)` ~~Strikethrough~~: `~~Strikethrough~~`
1. Ordered List 2. Item `3. Item`
- Unordered List - Item `- Item`
For more tips, visit [https://www.markdownguide.org/cheat-sheet/](https://www.markdownguide.org/cheat-sheet/)
